# 7-Day Book Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## Overview

This project showcases an **interactive 7-Day Book Sales Dashboard** developed in **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, product demand, and regional sales distribution. The dashboard provides business users with a clear and concise view of key sales metrics, enabling data-driven decision-making through interactive filters and visualizations.

The dashboard is designed to monitor sales activities over a seven-day period and offers insights into book performance, transaction volume, net sales, gross sales, and state-wise revenue contributions.

---

## Dashboard Objectives

The primary objectives of this dashboard are:

* Monitor overall sales performance for the last 7 days.
* Identify top-selling books and their revenue contribution.
* Track transaction volume and sales amounts.
* Analyze daily sales trends.
* Understand regional sales distribution.
* Provide interactive filtering for quick business analysis.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### 1. Number of Orders

Displays the total number of transactions completed during the reporting period.

**Value:** 52 Transactions

This KPI helps measure customer activity and sales volume.

### 2. Net Sales

Represents the actual revenue generated after adjustments.

**Value:** 45.50K

This metric provides insight into the effective revenue earned during the selected period.

### 3. Gross Sales

Represents the total sales amount before deductions.

**Value:** 53.69K

This KPI helps evaluate overall business performance and revenue generation.

## Dashboard Features

### Interactive Status Filters

The dashboard includes interactive buttons that allow users to filter records based on order status:

* Select All
* Failed
* Pending
* Success

These filters dynamically update all visuals across the dashboard, providing focused analysis.

## Visualizations

### 1. Books Sold (Top 5)

This bar chart displays the top five books based on the number of units sold.

**Insights:**

* Identifies the most popular books.
* Helps understand customer preferences.
* Supports inventory and marketing decisions.

### 2. Books Revenue (Top 5)

This visualization ranks books according to revenue generated.

**Insights:**

* Highlights the highest revenue-generating products.
* Helps prioritize profitable products.
* Assists in pricing and promotional strategies.

### 3. Day-Wise Sales Analysis

Displays net sales performance across individual days.

**Insights:**

* Tracks daily sales fluctuations.
* Identifies peak sales days.
* Supports forecasting and planning activities.

### 4. State-Wise Net Revenue Distribution

A pie chart representing revenue contribution from different states.

**States Included:**

* Maharashtra
* Bihar
* Delhi
* West Bengal
* Uttar Pradesh

**Insights:**

* Understands geographical sales performance.
* Identifies strong and weak markets.
* Supports regional business strategies.

### 5. Gross Amount by Day

This chart illustrates gross sales distribution across different dates.

**Insights:**

* Compares daily gross sales performance.
* Detects sales spikes and trends.
* Supports operational planning.
